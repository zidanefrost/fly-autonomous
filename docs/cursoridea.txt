AI OTP Optimisation Company

A Self Running AI Business for Airlines

Business Concept

The AI OTP Optimisation Company helps airlines improve On Time Performance (OTP), reduce delays and lower operational costs through a network of intelligent AI agents.

The company operates autonomously by analysing operational data, identifying delay risks, recommending solutions and generating reports for airline customers.

The objective is to create a business that can run with minimal human involvement while continuously delivering value to airlines.



Neuroscience Principles Applied

The AI system is inspired by how the human brain processes information and makes decisions.

1. Attention System

The human brain focuses on the most important information first.

AI Implementation

The Attention Agent continuously monitors flights and prioritises situations that have the greatest operational impact.

Example

A 15 minute delay on a low load factor flight receives low priority.

A 15 minute delay on a fully booked flight with many connecting passengers receives high priority.



2. Memory System

Humans learn from previous experiences and recognise patterns.

AI Implementation

The Memory Agent stores historical operational data and identifies recurring delay trends.

Example

The system learns that flights departing DXB for JED on Friday evenings experience recurring air traffic congestion.

The agent automatically factors this into future predictions.



3. Prediction System

The human brain constantly predicts future events.

AI Implementation

The Prediction Agent forecasts operational disruptions before they occur.

Predictions Include

ATC delays
Crew delays
Maintenance delays
Weather disruptions
Airport congestion
Turnaround delays
Example

Current Schedule:

Flight FZ123

Scheduled Departure: 14:00

AI Prediction:

82% probability of a 25 minute departure delay



4. Reward System

Humans make decisions by evaluating potential rewards and consequences.

AI Implementation

The Reward Agent evaluates all possible solutions and selects the action with the highest operational benefit.

Metrics Considered

OTP improvement
Cost savings
Passenger impact
Crew utilisation
Aircraft utilisation
